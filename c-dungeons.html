The Rebirth of Vecna: Dungeons and Dragons, 1495 DR

Welcome to the Dungeons & Dragons Committee! Our adventure unfolds in the vibrant world of Faerûn, with the Sword Coast as our primary stage. This region, a land of intrigue, danger, and boundless opportunity, will serve as the backdrop to your epic decisions and actions. Delegates will take on the roles of heroes, monsters, or enigmatic entities—each with their own ambitions, allegiances, and schemes. As the forces of evil rise to threaten the delicate balance of power. The Lords’ Alliance has summoned you to this crucial council. You have been chosen for your strength, influence, and reputation across the realms, whether as a champion of justice, a cunning manipulator, or a harbinger of chaos. This council is tasked with maintaining the fragile order of Faerûn, but not all seated here share the same vision. Some come with noble intentions, others with personal vendettas, and still others with secret agendas. Despite your differences, the Alliance hopes to unite this diverse assembly to counter the growing shadow and ensure the survival of the world as we know it. Will you join forces to defend Faerûn, exploit the chaos for your own gain, or carve a new destiny that reshapes the realms? The choice is yours, but beware—every decision will echo across the Sword Coast, and the fate of the land hangs in the balance. Prepare for diplomacy, betrayal, and adventure. The stage is set. The dice are ready. Let the game begin!
