The President's Ad Hoc

Reason and logic- two words I would not use to describe this committee. Even then, I am certain of one thing: your skills as a delegate will be tested to their limits. Delegates in this committee will deal with new mechanics forcing them to adapt on the go. Being a staple in the ad-hoc circuit, I've seen a thing or two about the ad-hoc experience. Understanding this all, our dais has crafted this one-of-a-kind committee which we hope you will enjoy. Like all other ad-hocs, we encourage delegates with more experience to participate in this committee due to its non-traditional elements, which requires a more advanced and seasoned understanding of the basics. Last but not least, we look forward to seeing you all in the burg soon!

- Vihaan, Crisis Director
