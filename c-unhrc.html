The Global Refugee Crisis Amid Rising Conflicts: UNHRC 2024

We are excited to welcome you to the Blacksburg International Relations & Diplomacy Model United Nations Conference (BIRDMUNC), hosted by the Model United Nations at Virginia Tech. Our conference brings together students from across the nation and the world to engage in debate, diplomacy, and problem-solving on the global stage. We hope that through your participation, you will build a deeper understanding of global issues and form lasting connections with other future diplomats.

The conference will be held on February 6th-9th 2025, and we look forward to seeing you there!
